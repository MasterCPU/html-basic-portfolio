The Best Movies according to Narendra

My top 3 best movies of all-time.

Sher Shivraj

When Maharashtra is being crushed under the tyrannical Adilshahi rule, Lord Narsimha appears to save them in the form of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj.

Ramayana

Ramayana: The Legend of Prince Rama is a 1992 Japanese-Indian co-produced anime film; Produced and directed by Yugo Sako. It is based on the Indian epic Ramayana. The film was directed by Koichi Sasaki and Ram Mohan, with music composed by Vanraj Bhatia.

Hanuman

After being granted immortality by Hindu deities such as his father Vaiu, the god of wind, Hanuman embarks on an epic journey.
